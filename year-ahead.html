Planning

There are also a lot of things that I’m coming to understand we view really differently. It’s been no surprise that relationships take effort and I think we’ve always been good at working to make ours so good. That said, we are very different people in ways that I’m still coming to appreciate. In our time in Thailand your friend group was the guys and you were 1000% comfortable with them. There are plenty of things like this where I’m still working to understand the way we deal with tasks and situations, and the first one that comes to mind is planning. There are very few things in life that I will plan extensively for (as you’ve come to see), and travel is certainly one of those things. In an absolutely perfect world for me I would wake up, decide what to do on that day and that day alone, and then I’d go to sleep and repeat that the following day.

We’ve already gone over the issue of planning visits out over the span of a week or so, and I think that’s going better, but I know you focus much more than I do on the future so you end up doing a lot of the planning for things that we do together. I don’t like reaching the point at which I’d start planning a trip (say, one week before we leave for Portugal) only to find that you’ve planned the whole trip without any help from me. Be It something that big or just random time we have together I can’t promise to always plan stuff because that would probably drive me crazy lol, but I really will work to be better at doing my half of the planning if nothing else.

Presents

I certainly like getting them and giving them feels nice, but I think there are other ways to prove you care that don’t involve presents. I know you don’t disagree with that at all, but I think you like giving them more and so you’ll do all of your shopping in November and I’ll do mine after final exams end on December 18th or whatever.

This past Christmas having bought the ring I was planning on smaller stuff but I was super surprised to find that I had actually only given you a couple small things. I do love giving them to you and to other people and so I’ll be working to try and avoid a situation like that in the future.
