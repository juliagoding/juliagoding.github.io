12 Days of Us

In no particular order, my 12 favorite days, places, and memories of ours

The Houseboat

I can't forget the 'o shit' feeling that followed you asking me what I'd think about joining you on this houseboat trip in case Gunner ended up backing out (I guess by then you knew him well enough to see that coming). I had absolutely no idea what to expect and we had been on our first date exactly one week prior. I knew I liked you and that we were falling quickly into something but this trip would make me face that and acknowledge to myself that this was a serious thing. I thought I wanted it to be, but with it being so fast and unexpected I just wasn't positive yet. After a fun and natural-feeling day of SongKran the only uncertainty left was what exactly 'houseboat' meant here.

I can't exactly remember how or why but somehow the ride down up to the lake was surprisingly dry. Vision made the trip just fine and I was relieved that my cell signal gave us google maps the whole way. I remember just being impressed with your Thai since I had heard you speak it well but I'd never counted on it before and I certainly did then.

From the time we arrived at the house to the time we left (on a 3-hours-late boat) I felt as happy relaxed and comfortable as I ever had at the cabin. Losing to you at chess was tough to swallow but turned me on almost as much as my solving of a rubics cube turned you on.

Of course the ride home was an advendure of its own, and seeing you try to distract me and cheer me up was the cherry on top that made me feel confident that, even though things were moving fast, there was reason for it.
